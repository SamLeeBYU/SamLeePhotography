Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a session?

  • To book a session, simply select the package that fits your needs and click “Book Now.” You’ll be redirected to the contact form to schedule your session.

What is your deposit policy?

  • A 20% deposit is required to confirm the booking, with the balance due on the day of the session.

When will I receive my photos?

  • You can expect your edited photos within 2 weeks after the session.
