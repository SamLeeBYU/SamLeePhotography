About Sam Lee

Sam Lee is an independent photographer based in Orem, UT. His journey into photography began with a fascination for the beauty in everyday moments, which quickly grew into a freelance career specializing in urban, landscape, and portrait photography.

Sam’s work reflects his belief that every photograph tells a story—from the mountainous landscapes of Korea or the intimate emotions captured in candid portraits. He’s passionate about capturing the world the world as it turly is, a style often referred to as photojournalism. Sam excels at capturing moments that tend to go unnoticed. He continues to push creative boundaries, producing work that resonates with viewers on a deeper level.

Beyond photography, Sam is currently a pursing a Masters of Science in Statistics at Brigham Young University where he hopes to continue a PhD program in economics with a specialization in econometrics soon after. When Sam is not studying for exams or outside taking photos, he enjoys reading, hiking, trying new foods, playing tennis, or keeping up with his latest series on Netflix.

Feel free to explore his portfolio and reach out for inquiries or collaboration opportunities.
